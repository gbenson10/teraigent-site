Why traditional syncing fails

Connecting Salesforce and HubSpot is a notoriously brittle process. Traditional iPaaS solutions like Zapier or Make break when custom fields change, and native integrations often lack the logic needed to handle complex routing, lead scoring, or deduplication.

The Agentic Approach

Teraigent deploys an autonomous workflow agent that doesn't just pass data back and forth. It understands the context of the data. When a lead reaches a specific stage in HubSpot, the agent intelligently provisions the account in Salesforce, enriching the record with external data and notifying the assigned AE—all without human intervention.

Core Capabilities

  • Intelligent deduplication across both platforms using fuzzy matching.
  • Real-time bidirectional sync that survives custom field schema changes.
  • Automated lead enrichment before routing to Salesforce.
  • Error handling and self-healing pipelines that notify you only when human judgment is required.
